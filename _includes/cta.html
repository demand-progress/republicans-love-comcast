Tell Republican Lawmakers: Stop shilling for Big Cable

Americans overwhelmingly support Net Neutrality, but several dozen Republicans who sit on the committees that oversee the Federal Communications Commission are trying to kill it. They just signed a letter* in opposition to President Obama’s proposal to protect the Internet — and one of them even called Net Neutrality “Obamacare for the Internet”! They’re just doing the bidding of Comcast, AT&T, Time Warner, and the other big cable monopolies — who, incidentally, have donated hundreds thousands of dollars to their campaigns.
